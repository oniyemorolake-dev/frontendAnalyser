Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

What we collect

When you use MoTechCo Resume Analyzer, you may upload a resume file, paste a job description, enter an email address, or pay for a premium report. Resume files are processed to extract text and are not permanently stored on our servers after processing.

How we use data

Extracted resume text is sent to AI services to generate feedback. Payment information is handled securely by Stripe. If you request an emailed report, we use your email only to deliver that report and optional MoTechCo updates if you opt in later.

What we do not do

We do not sell your resume content. We do not permanently store uploaded files after text extraction completes.

Third parties

We use hosting providers (Render, Netlify), payment processing (Stripe), and AI providers (Google Gemini) to operate the service.

Contact

Questions about privacy: contact mowebsiteco@gmail.com.
