About us

Built for people who are tired of sending applications into the void

MoTechCo started as a simple idea: give job seekers a honest score, show what's missing for this job, and help them put together one strong application — without paying $30 a month for tools they'll forget to cancel.

What we believe

Your resume should reflect your real experience. We never invent employers, degrees, or skills you don't have. Every rewrite, cover letter, and interview hint is grounded in what you actually uploaded.

We built the application kit around one job at a time — because that's how most people apply. One posting. One tailored package. One fair price.

Who it's for

Recent grads, career changers, nurses, tech workers, remote applicants, and anyone applying in Canada or internationally who wants clear feedback before they hit submit.
